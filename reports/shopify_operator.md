## Your 30-day bet

> If we automate drafting initial customer support replies via a Gmail integration, we will reduce the time spent on support emails by 40% within 30 days.

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Target workflow** | `customer-support-management` |
| **Success metric** | Reduce time spent on customer support emails to under 180 minutes per week (40% reduction from your stated 300 minutes average). |
| **Failure metric** | Time spent on customer support emails remains above 270 minutes per week (less than 10% reduction from your stated 300 minutes average). |

4. **Week 4** — Evaluate time savings and adjust templates for improved efficiency.
