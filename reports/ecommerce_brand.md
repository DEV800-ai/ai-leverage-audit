## Your 30-day bet

### Automate Responses to Common Shipping Questions

> If we automate responses to common shipping questions using a chatbot integrated with Shopify, we will see a 40% reduction in time spent on customer support within 30 days.

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Target workflow** | `customer-support` |
| **Success metric** | Reduce time spent on customer support to under 108 minutes per week (40% reduction from your stated 180 minutes average). |
| **Failure metric** | Time spent on customer support remains above 162 minutes per week (10% reduction from your stated 180 minutes average). |
| **Time investment** | 3h/wk (within your 5h/wk budget) |
| **Setup cost** | $50 (within your $200/mo budget) |
| **Expected asset by day 30** | A fully integrated chatbot on Shopify that automatically responds to common shipping questions. |

#### Weekly plan

1. **Week 1** — Identify and categorize the top 5 most common shipping questions from past customer inquiries.
2. **Week 2** — Draft and refine automated response templates for each identified question.
3. **Week 3** — Integrate the chatbot with Shopify and test the automated responses internally.
4. **Week 4** — Launch the chatbot to handle live customer inquiries and monitor performance.

#### First 48 hours — start here

- [ ] Block 30 minutes on calendar to review past customer inquiries for common shipping questions.
- [ ] List the top 5 most frequently asked shipping questions from memory.
- [ ] Bookmark Shopify's chatbot integration documentation for quick access.
