## Where AI can save you the most time

### #1 — Guest Research & Outreach · _confidence: high_
_AI can streamline guest research, saving significant time._

- Estimated time saved: **~3.0h/wk**
- Risk if AI gets it wrong: medium · Setup complexity: medium · Human judgment needed: high
- Suggested mix: **50% automate** / 20% assist / **30% keep human**
- Evidence from your intake:
  - _Finding and researching good guests_
  - _Writing personalized outreach messages_
  - Automate: Identify potential guests, Draft outreach emails
  - Assist: Suggest guest topics
  - Keep human: Finalize guest list

### Automate Initial Guest Identification

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Target workflow** | `guest-research-outreach` |

| **Failure metric** | No reduction in guest research time or decrease in guest quality. |
| **Time investment** | 3h/wk (within your 4h/wk budget) |
| **Setup cost** | $50 (within your $100/mo budget) |
| **Expected asset by day 30** | A streamlined process for initial guest identification using AI tools. |

1. **Week 1** — Identify and test AI tools for guest identification.
2. **Week 2** — Implement AI tool and begin using it for guest identification.
3. **Week 3** — Monitor and adjust AI tool settings based on feedback.
4. **Week 4** — Evaluate time saved and guest quality, and decide on continued use.
