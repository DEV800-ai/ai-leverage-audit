| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Target workflow** | `lead-management` |

| **Failure metric** | No reduction in response time or increase in customer complaints about initial contact within 30 days. |
| **Time investment** | 3h/wk (within your 3h/wk budget) |
| **Setup cost** | $0 (within your $100/mo budget) |
| **Expected asset by day 30** | A functional automated initial reply system for WhatsApp inquiries, with documented response time improvements. |

2. **Week 2** — Implement the automated reply using WhatsApp Business features.
3. **Week 3** — Monitor response times and gather feedback from new leads.
4. **Week 4** — Analyze data and adjust the template or process as needed.

## Areas to keep human-controlled

- **Serious customer complaints** _(severity: high)_ — Requires empathy and nuanced judgment.
- **Final discounts or refunds** _(severity: medium)_ — Financial decisions need human oversight.
- **Firing or replacing cleaners** _(severity: high)_ — Sensitive HR matter.
- **Entering homes without instructions** _(severity: high)_ — Security and trust issue.
